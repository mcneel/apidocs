double Tolerance

  • (summary) Gets or sets the accuracy of the edge curve (>=0.0 or RhinoMath.UnsetValue) A value of UnsetValue indicates that the tolerance should be computed.

    The maximum distance from the edge’s 3d curve to any surface of a face that has this edge as a portion of its boundary must be <= this tolerance.

  • (since) 5.0
