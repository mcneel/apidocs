ColorCMYK
Published: 0001-01-01

Namespace: Rhino.Display

Represents a CMYK (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Key) color with double precision floating point channels. CMYK colors are used primarily in printing environments as they provide a good simulation of physical ink.

Constructors

ColorCMYK(double cyan,double magenta,double yellow)

  • (summary) Initializes a new instance of ColorCMYK with custom channel values. The cyan, magenta and yellow values will be adjusted based on their combined darkness.
